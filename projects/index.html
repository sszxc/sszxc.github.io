Markerless Motion Capture with a Single Video Camera

Advised by Prof. Yangang Wang Nov.2018 - Nov.2019 • Used a CNN-based method to predict the 2D body joints from images and then fitted a statistical body shape model, called SMPL, to the 2D joints • Converted the project into an optimization problem with an objective function that penalized the error between the projected 3D model joints and detected 2D joints • Displayed a poster in Student Session of 2019 IEEE International Workshop on Signal Processing Systems (SIPS) • Won a prize for outstanding work in Student Research Training Program of Jiangsu Province

Deep Q-network for Learning of Robotic Grasp

Advised by Prof. Jun Li Dec.2019 - Present • Utilized supervised learning to initialize the weights of Q-network so as to guide robotic grasp at correct regions • Exploited a fairly small coarse-grained dataset to pre-train the Q-network and reduces manually labeling burden
